About me

"I kept learning many new skills and technologies and that desire has never faded, even to this day."

As you may already know, my name is Michael Rawlings. I'm a twenty-two (22) years old young man from Frankfort, Kentucky. I love football, no matter what. If you must know, I support Philadelphia Eagles (NFL) and University of Kentucky Wildcats (college football and basketball).

I'm a avid gamer, having played many games ranging from old and classic (Battletoad!) to modern games, but I prefer PC over console. Ever since I first got on my dad's computer when I was about 6 years old, I insantly became addicted to it. No matter what, I always had that desire to learn more about it, I was able to teach myself HTML (back when netscape existed). I kept learning many new skills and technology and that desire has never faded, even to this day. Because of that day, this is why I'm currently a third year software engineering student at Rochester Institute of Technology intending to graudate in May 2016. Also, I have few personal projects (more on that in projects page), this website being one of them.
